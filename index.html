About al‑Kindi

A small, focused studio with roots in software and a strong foundation in design.

We prefer clear scope, honest dialogue and work that can stand on its own without big words. Sometimes that means adding a feature; sometimes it means simplifying and removing noise.

We're not chasing trends. We're here to help you build something useful, consistent and well‑made.
